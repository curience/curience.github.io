The Scoop

A social media search engine dedicated to hashtags.

Hshtags serves to be a hub for hashtagged posts across a variety of social media platforms. Currently we aggregate posts from Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Tumblr, Youtube, Vimeo and Flickr. A new user does not need an official account to search but with an account, a user will also be able to see private posts from persons within their own network on each respective platform (that they have authorized). They are also given the opportunity to follow and keep track of their favorite tags, organize them into lists and follow lists others have created.

The main benefit of Hshtags is to give people the ability to search across multiple platforms, in one tab. Whether they have an account on the platform or not, we give them the chance to discover and follow conversations surrounding their favorite hashtags.
