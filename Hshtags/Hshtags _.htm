How Tos

Search

How do I search for a tag?
Start typing to open search or find it in the side navigation. Search for a tag by selecting or typing the '#' symbol.
Why are some posts missing from the feed?
  • We can only return public posts from each platform. If a user's post/profile is private you can only see their content if you are connected to them and have also authenticated that platform within your account.
  • At this time, we only get 2 weeks worth of historical Twitter data.

Advanced Search (Power Plan)

How do I use 'And Hashtags'?
And hashtags give you the ability to add another hashtag to the search to be more precise.
How do I use 'Or Hashtags'?
Or hashtags provide alternatives to the main tag search.
How do I use keywords?
By adding keywords or phrases to your search, you are able to surface more relevant results. Only posts with one or more of the words provided will be returned.
What is Relevancy Percent?
By providing a relevancy percent threshold, search is more precise in returning results with low or high relevancy percentages based on the keywords provided.
How do I use 'Include Platforms'?
Only content from the platforms selected will be returned in the results feed.

Saved Searches (Power Plan)

How do I save a search?
When conducting a search, you will have the option to save the search and all the options/filters selected.
Where can I find all of my saved searches?
Your saved searches can be accessed by opening search and hitting the 'saved searches' button to load all your previously saved searches or by clicking the link in the side navigation.

Tag Subscriptions (Power Plan)

How do subscribe to a tag?
You can either subscribe to a tag by clicking the icon beside the tag name on a search feed or on the main tag subscriptions page which can be accessed from your side navigation.
Where can I find all of my tag subscriptions?
Your tag subscriptions can be accessed by clicking the 'Tag Subscriptions' link in the side navigation.

Saved Posts (Power Plan)

How do I save a post?
Each post in the results feed will have a save option displayed at the bottom of the post. Clicking this will save the post to your bucket.
Where can I find all of my saved posts?
Your saved posts can be accessed by clicking the 'Saved Posts' link in the side navigation.

Account

How do I change my default homepage?
You can change you default homepage on the settings page. It can either be a list or a single tag.
How can I authorize more networks?
You may authorize more networks on the settings page.
How can upgrade to the Power Plan?
You can upgrade to the Power Plan from the settings page in your account.
How do I delete my account?
You may delete your account on the settings page.
